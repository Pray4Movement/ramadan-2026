## Style Guidelines

1. **Scripture quotations**: Use an established Bible translation in each language  (e.g., Louis Segond for French (FRALSG), Reina-Valera for Spanish (SPARVC), Italian Riveduta 1927 for Italian (ITAR27), NAV for Arabic (ARBIBS), Almeida Revista e Atualizada for Portuguese (PORARA))

2. **Consistency**: Once you choose a term, use it consistently throughout

3. **Capitalization**: Follow target language conventions for religious terms

4. **Names**: Use the standard biblical names in each language tradition

5. **Prayer points**: Maintain the imperative/exhortative tone ("Pray that...", "Ask God to...")

6. **Cultural sensitivity**: The content discusses Islam respectfully while presenting Christian faith - maintain this tone

7. **Bullet points**: Preserve the bullet point structure (lines starting with "-")

## Questions?

If you're unsure about a term, check:
- An established Bible translation in your language
- Christian resources from your language/region
- Consult with native speaker Christians familiar with both Islamic and Christian terminology
